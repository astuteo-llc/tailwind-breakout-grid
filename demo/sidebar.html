Getting Started with CNC-5000

Everything you need to set up and operate your new 5-axis machining center.

Before You Begin

Ensure your facility meets all power and environmental requirements. See the Installation Guide for complete site preparation details.

Overview

The CNC-5000 Series represents the pinnacle of 5-axis machining technology. This guide will walk you through initial setup, basic operation, and best practices for achieving optimal results.

Whether you're migrating from a 3-axis system or upgrading from an older 5-axis machine, you'll find the CNC-5000's intuitive interface and advanced automation features significantly reduce your learning curve.

Quick Start Checklist

Follow these steps to get your machine operational. Each step links to detailed documentation.

1

Verify Power Connection

Confirm 480V 3-phase supply and proper grounding.

2

Check Coolant System

Fill reservoir and verify pump operation.

3

Initialize Control System

Power on and complete startup sequence.

4

Home All Axes

Establish machine zero position.

Safety Warning

Never operate the machine with safety interlocks bypassed. Always ensure the work area is clear before initiating any axis movement.
