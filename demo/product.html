Key Features

Built for precision, designed for productivity.

ThermoLock Temperature Compensation

Our proprietary thermal management system continuously monitors and compensates for temperature variations across all axes. Real-time adjustments ensure consistent accuracy regardless of ambient conditions or machine warm-up state.

Direct-Drive Rotary Axes

Eliminate backlash and maximize responsiveness with our direct-drive A and C axes. Achieve superior surface finishes on complex contours while reducing cycle times by up to 30% compared to traditional worm-gear designs.

Intelligent Tool Management

The 60-tool automatic changer with tool life monitoring ensures uninterrupted production. Predictive algorithms schedule tool changes during non-cutting moves, maximizing spindle utilization.
